# Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard

Power BI dashboard analyzing sales performance, customer behavior, and product profitability using interactive visualizations and advanced DAX measures.

---

## Project Overview

This project analyzes sales data to identify key business insights such as revenue trends, customer purchasing behavior, and product performance.  
The dashboard enables decision-makers to explore sales performance through interactive visualizations and drill-through analysis.

---

## Tools Used

- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Data Modeling (Star Schema)

---

## Data Model

The project uses a **star schema** with the following tables:

Fact Table
- Sales

Dimension Tables
- Customers
- Products
- Date

This structure enables efficient analytical queries and time-based analysis.

---

## Key Metrics

The dashboard tracks several important business metrics:

- Total Revenue
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin %
- Total Orders
- Revenue per Customer
- Orders per Customer
- Rolling 12 Revenue
- Year-to-Date Revenue
- Month-over-Month Growth

---

## Dashboard Pages

### Home Page
Executive overview showing key KPIs and quick navigation to analysis pages.

### Sales Overview
Analyzes overall sales performance including:
- Revenue trends
- Top performing products
- Revenue by country
- Profit by product categories

### Customer Insights
Examines customer behavior including:
- Revenue per customer
- Orders per customer
- New vs repeat customers
- Customer segmentation by gender and country

### Product Performance
Evaluates product success through:
- Top profitable products
- Product margins
- Revenue vs quantity analysis
- Worst performing products

### Operations / Time Analysis
Time-based analysis including:
- Rolling 12 revenue
- Revenue YTD
- Monthly and quarterly sales trends
- Seasonality analysis

### Drill Pages
Detailed drill-through analysis for:
- Individual customers
- Individual products

---

## Key Insights

- A small number of products contribute the majority of total revenue.
- Profit margins improved over time, indicating improved operational efficiency.
- Sales show seasonal patterns across months and quarters.
- Customer purchasing behavior varies significantly across regions.
